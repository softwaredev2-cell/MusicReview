God Said No

By: Omar Apollo

About the album: This is an album that explores the emotional aftermath of a difficult relationship, focusing on themes of heartbreak, sorrow, and the search for healing. The album is described as a "survey of the emotional wreckage" and is intended to be listened to in sequence, allowing the listener to follow Apollo's journey through pain and eventual acceptance.

Withered

About the album: WITHERED was primarily created on BandLab, where David poured his heart and soul into every track. Through its haunting melodies and powerful lyrics, WITHERED explores the complex emotions of grief, loss, confusion, and love-emotions that David has lived through and now invites listeners to experience with him.
